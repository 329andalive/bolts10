3. Description of the Service

  • Proposal and invoice generation and delivery via SMS
  • Post-job review request automation
  • Job scheduling and dispatch management
  • Role-based team access (owner, foreman, field tech, office staff)

Before submitting an End Customer's mobile phone number to the Bolts11 platform, the Client must have obtained consent that meets the following minimum standards:
