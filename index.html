Simple by Design

Three steps. Zero learning curve.

Bolts11 plugs into how you already run your jobs. Your crew texts job updates the same way they always have — Bolts11 handles the rest.

1

You Book the Job

Schedule a job via your dispatch system or just text in the job details. Bolts11 captures everything automatically.

2

Bolts11 Notifies Your Customer

Your customer gets a professional SMS — confirmed appointment time, tech name, and what to expect. Automatically.

3

Job Done, Customer Happy

On completion, a follow-up SMS goes out — receipt, review request, or whatever fits your workflow.

Multi-Tenant Platform
