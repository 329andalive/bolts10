Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I stop receiving text messages?

Reply STOP to any text you received. Your number will be removed immediately — no login required. You can also email us at support@bolts11.com.

Who sent me a text?

Texts sent through Bolts11 come from a phone number registered to a specific trade business (plumber, HVAC company, septic service, etc.). The business name is included in the message. Reply STOP to stop their messages.

How do I sign my trade business up?

Fill out the contact form above or email support@bolts11.com. We'll schedule a demo and walk you through setup. Takes about 10 minutes.

Is there an app I need to download?

No. Bolts11 is entirely SMS-based. Your crew texts job updates from any phone. Your customers receive plain text messages. No apps. No portals. No passwords.

What trades does Bolts11 support?

Any field service trade: plumbing, HVAC, septic, sewer & drain, excavation, electrical, heating & oil, and more. If you do service work and drive to customers, Bolts11 can help.

Is my data private?

Yes. We never sell or share customer mobile numbers. All data is isolated per client. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
