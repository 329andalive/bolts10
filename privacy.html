Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

What We Collect

Bolts11 collects only the information needed to deliver SMS job notifications on behalf of the trade businesses that use our platform:

  • Customer phone numbers — provided by the trade business to send job notifications
  • Customer names and addresses — used to personalize messages and route jobs
  • Business owner contact info — phone, email, and business name for account management
  • SMS message content — job updates, invoices, proposals, and confirmations

How We Use It

All data is used exclusively to operate the Bolts11 platform for the trade business that provided it. We use customer data to:

  • Send transactional SMS notifications (appointment confirmations, job updates, invoices)
  • Generate proposals and invoices on behalf of the business
  • Maintain job history and scheduling records

What We Never Do

  • We never sell phone numbers, customer data, or message content to third parties
  • We never send marketing messages to customers — all SMS is transactional
  • We never share data between trade businesses — each account is fully isolated

SMS Opt-Out

Any customer can reply STOP to any message from Bolts11 to immediately opt out of all future SMS. This is honored instantly and permanently unless the customer re-subscribes by replying START.

Data Storage

All data is stored in encrypted databases hosted by Supabase (PostgreSQL). Access is restricted to authenticated platform users only. We do not store credit card numbers — payments are processed through Square.

Data Retention

Job records, messages, and customer data are retained for as long as the trade business maintains an active Bolts11 account. Upon account cancellation, data is deleted within 90 days.
